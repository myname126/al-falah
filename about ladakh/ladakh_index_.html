History of Ladakh

Ladakh has a rich history influenced by Tibetan and Indian cultures. It was an important stop on the ancient Silk Route.

Festivals of Ladakh

Hemis Festival is one of the most famous celebrations, featuring traditional masked dances.

Losar Festival marks the Tibetan New Year with grand festivities.

Ladakh Festival showcases the vibrant culture, music, and dance of the region.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit Ladakh is from May to September.

Acclimatization is important due to the high altitude; stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activities on the first day.

Carry warm clothing even in summer, as temperatures drop significantly at night.
